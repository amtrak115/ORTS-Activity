MSTS Milwaukee Boxcar Pack 

Nine  Milwaukee Road photo skinned  50 & 60' Boxcars with updated Ted Curphey's shape files for better trucks, 
hoses and more detail. Original files and textures by Ted "Get well Soon" Curphey. Repaints by Dean Kurowski
of Sceeter's Paint Shop.

Installation:

    1.  Unzip the contents of this file to your Trainset folder(Drive:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Train Simulator\Trains\Trainset)
        (or wherever you may keep your MSTS)
    2.  Create a consist and have at it!
