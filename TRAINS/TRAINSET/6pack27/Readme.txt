MSTS Set of six 50' boxcars for SP 245479, 248074, 248292, 248565, 651679 and 670343. Original model and textures by Ted Curphey. Truck and coupler texture by Jeff Auberpine. New car end textures and photo repaints by Mark Giltz.  Photos provided by and used with the permission of Robert Pisani, John Huey, and Glenn Laux.

email: markuprailfan@aol.com

APRIL 2009

Installation:

Unzip to your Trainset folder; a folder named "6PACK27" will be created and the files added to it.  Use the activity editor or ConBuilder to add the carS to the Sim.  The cars will be listed as "boxcar 50' SP245479 Loaded", etc.
