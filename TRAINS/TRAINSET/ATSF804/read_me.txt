MSTS-ATSF C40-8W #804 is a re-skin of NALW's C40-8W with new engine physics and lighting by Bob Boudoin.Re-skinned using the author's photos. By TKW and Pete Abrams. Renumber and modifications by Bob Boudoin. All the good stuff is by Pete, all the mistakes are by Bob. 
The number boards are not in the correct position for the prototype, and the number font is not perfect, but it is the best I have.

ATSF C40-8W #804

Engine		7FDL16
Height		15' 6"
Length		70' 6"  
Width		10' 2.7  
Power		4,000 hp 
Weight		200.72 U.S. tons  
Max. Speed	77 mph  


HOW TO USE This engine:

These cars are ready to use. To install it do the following:

1. Unzip the contents of ATSF804 into Trainsim/trains/trainset folder. That's it create a consist and go.
