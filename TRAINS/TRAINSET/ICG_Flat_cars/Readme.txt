MSTS ICG flat car pack
Original model by Teemu Saukkonen
Some fonts by Pete Willard
Reskin by P. Tyler Bundy
Physics by Turbo Bill

Reskins of Enocell's EL flatcar pack into ICG and predecessor paint schemes.

Installation
-------------------------

Unzip file to temporary location.  Move 'ICG_Flat_cars' folders to your 'TRAINSET' folder.  Create consist and enjoy!
